What is PittHub?

PittHub is an open community for Panthers — a shared space to connect, play, and discover. Explore Pitt-themed games and tools built by students, for students.

Who can join?

Every Panther is welcome. Create a profile, opt in to the community, and take part in daily games, matchers, and experiences shaped by the Pitt student body.

What's next for PittHub?

PittHub keeps growing. New community features, games, and tools are added over time to keep the hub fresh and useful for everyone at Pitt.
